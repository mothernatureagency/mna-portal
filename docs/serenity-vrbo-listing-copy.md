## Description

Welcome to Serenity on the Bay, a one-of-a-kind bayfront vacation home on 1.5 private acres in Freeport, Florida. Whether you are planning a family vacation, a friends getaway, or a couples retreat, this home delivers the kind of waterfront experience you cannot find in a hotel or condo.

**The Property**
Step inside to soaring cathedral ceilings, warm hardwood floors, and a double-sided fireplace that anchors the open living space. The kitchen features smart appliances and everything you need to cook a full meal without leaving the house. Four bedrooms and 2.5 bathrooms sleep up to 10 guests comfortably, with a king suite, two queen rooms, and a bunk room that kids will love.

**The Primary Suite**
Your spa-style retreat features an LED mirror, a walk-in rainfall shower, and a stunning fluted soaking tub. Close the door, light a candle, and reset.

**The Outdoor Space**
This is where Serenity shines. Step outside to 1.5 acres of bayfront property with your own private dock. Fish right off the dock, launch kayaks and paddleboards, or just sit with a drink and watch the dolphins cruise by at sunset. The fire pit is perfect for s'mores after a long day on the water. Ample parking and a cargo elevator make loading in groceries and gear easy.

**The Location**
Freeport sits perfectly between Destin and 30A, giving you access to the best of the Emerald Coast without the crowds. You are 15 minutes from Destin Harbor, Crab Island, and Henderson Beach State Park. Restaurants, shopping, and boat rentals are all a short drive away.

**Guest Favorites**
Guests love the private dock, the peaceful bay views, the spa bathroom, and how much space the property offers compared to typical vacation rentals. This is not a cookie-cutter beach condo. This is your own bayfront retreat.
